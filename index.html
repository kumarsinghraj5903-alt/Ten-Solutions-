About Ten Solutions

Ten Solutions is a completely free, student-first EdTech platform built to support CBSE students of Classes 9 to 12 in their academic journey, especially those preparing for board exams in 2025-26 and beyond.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves access to high-quality, reliable study resources without any cost, ads, or hidden barriers. Our goal is to simplify CBSE learning and help you score better with confidence.

What We Offer

  • Direct NCERT Books – Official viewer links to open textbooks instantly
  • Textbook Solutions – Chapter-wise NCERT solutions from trusted sources
  • Sample Papers (2020–2026) – Latest CBSE SQPs + full archive
  • Previous Year Papers – Actual board exam papers with pattern analysis
  • Revision Notes – New & updated concise notes for quick revision
  • AI Assistant – 24/7 smart helper that answers almost any NCERT question in detail
  • Support – Direct email connect to the founder for any help

Why Choose Ten Solutions?

  • 100% Free – No subscription, no ads, no paywalls
  • Curated from Official CBSE/NCERT & Trusted Educational Sites
  • Designed for Indian Students – Helpful for CBSE schools across the country
  • Mobile-Friendly – Works perfectly on phone, tablet & laptop
  • Regular Updates – Content aligned with latest 2025-26 syllabus & pattern
  • Student-Centric – Built by someone who understands board exam pressure
